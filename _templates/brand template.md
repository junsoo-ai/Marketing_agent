## Overview

**CLYPT** is an end-to-end infrastructure for systematic investing. We enable anyone to build, validate, deploy, and access investment strategies through a single system. Instead of treating trading as execution, we treat it as a structured process — from idea to capital.

> Code becomes capital. Process becomes proof.

### Ownership & Control

### Clarity over Noise

## Logo System

### Logo Mark
Built from **eight modular units** arranged in a radial system, creating a sense of balance and controlled movement.

**Construction principles:**
- Radial Construction
- Modular blocks
- Directional Cut

### Full Logo
The logotype is paired with the symbol to create a clear and stable identity across all brand applications.

### System Marks
The system expands into multiple marks, each representing a different layer of the CLYPT ecosystem:
- **Clypt** (main)
- **Engine**
- **Marketplace** — Outward Module Distribution, Radial Expansion, Decentralized Flow Pattern
- **Prediction Market** — Converging Radial Geometry, Centralized Signal Structure, Outer Octagon to Inner Core
- **Symbol / Text**

### Logo Scaling (Symbol sizes)
| Size | Use |
|------|-----|
| 185px | Large applications |
| 124px | Standard |
| 83px | Medium |
| 56px | Small |
| 22px | Minimum / Favicon |

### Clear Space
Maintain clearance of **x** (symbol width) on all sides of the logo.

### Logo Usage Specs (px)

| Context | Symbol | Total |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Landing page | — | 31 × 31, 34, 76 |
| Workspace sidebar | 22 × 22 | — |
| Favicon | 31 × 31 | 12, 36, 118 |
| Small | 22 × 22 | 11, 25, 87 |
| Icon box | 24 × 24 | 32 × 32 box |

### Logo Don'ts
- Do NOT rotate the symbol
- DO NOT COMPRESS
- Do not alter the visual style or stroke
- DO NOT INCREASE SYMBOL SPACING
- Do not distort or modify proportions
- DO NOT STACK SYMBOL ABOVE LOGOTYPE
- Do not violate clear space requirements
- DO NOT APPLY COLOR TO SYMBOL
