**Gold rebounds toward $4,867 as yields ease slightly on US–Iran optimism**
Gold recovered toward the $4,867 level as 10-year Treasury yields pulled back modestly alongside oil prices, with the potential US–Iran peace deal reducing the safe-haven urgency that had previously compressed gold amid high real yields. Major bank consensus for year-end 2026 gold ranges from Goldman Sachs at $5,400 to J.P. Morgan and Wells Fargo at $6,300. *(Crux Investor, May 15, 2026; goldsilver.com analyst survey)*
